Dining and Drinks

Infinity Pool Lounge

Feel revitalized with a refreshing dip in our outdoor infinity pool, located at the terrace and providing panoramic views over the Corniche and the Mediterranean Sea. Indulge in our delectable à la carte menu that offers a range of delightful dishes and choose from our variety of menu items including salads, snacks and sandwiches, pizza or pasta and other mouthwatering dishes. Finish your meal with a mouthwatering desserts, overlooking the marvelous view of the Mediterranean Sea.

La Gourmandise Salon De The

Enjoy inspired French cuisine in inviting surroundings at La Gourmandise French Pastry and Tea Lounge at Hilton Alexandria Corniche. Relax in the idyllic atmosphere of our traditional French tea lounge and admire the sweeping sea views while savoring a coffee and croissant. The delicious menu also includes cakes, pastries, pizzas, light meals, salads and sandwiches, accompanied by a full selection of hot and cold drinks.

Sahar EL-Laialy

TDine in this elegant Lebanese restaurant with stunning views over the Mediterranean coastline. Take your seat and indulge in a delicious dinner with mouth-watering Middle Eastern delicacies. Sample exotic delights such as warakenab, traditional lamb from the grill accompanied by a choice of tempting wines.

The Cigar Bar

After a hard day’s work enjoy your drink or favorite cocktail in our Cigar Bar. Our contemporary bar is serving light meals, snacks and refreshments all day. The bar is renowned for the wide selection of famous cigar brands.
